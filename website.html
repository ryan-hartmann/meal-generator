pie

  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 3 tablespoons cornstarch
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • 1 ½ cups water
  • 2 lemons, juiced and zested
  • 2 teaspoons butter
  • 4 egg yolks, beaten
  • 19-inch pie crust, baked
  • 4 egg whites, beaten
  • 6 teaspoons white sugar

  • Preheat oven: Preheat oven to 350°F
  • Make Lemon Filling: In a medium saucepan ...
    • Whisk together 1 cup sugar, flour, cornstarch, and salt.
    • Stir in water, lemon juice and lemon zest.
    • Cook over medium heat, stirring frequently, until mixtures comes to a boil.
    • Stir in butter.
    • Place egg yolks in a small boel and gradually whisk in ½ cup of hot sugar mix.
    • Bring to boil and continue to cook while stirring constantly until thick.
    • Remove from heat.
    • Pour filling into backed pir crust.
  • Make Meringue: In a large glass or metal bowl ...
    • Whip egg whites until foamy.
    • Add sugar gradually, and continue to whip until stiff peaks form.
    • Spread meringue over pie, sealing edges at the crust.
  • Bake: Bake in preheated oven for 10 minutes
